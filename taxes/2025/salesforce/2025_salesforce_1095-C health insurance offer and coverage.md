## Key Notes for Tax Filing

- **Do not attach this form to your tax return.** The 1095-C is for your records only.
- This form is used to verify that you had employer-sponsored health coverage and to determine eligibility for a Premium Tax Credit (PTC) on the Marketplace. Since coverage was through an employer (Salesforce) for Jan–Apr, you were likely not eligible for a PTC during those months.
- Coverage ended after April (Line 14 switches to code 1H and Line 16 switches to code 2A starting in May), meaning the family left Salesforce coverage as of May 2025. If Marketplace coverage was obtained for any months from May onward, Form 8962 may be relevant.
- The self-insured coverage in Part III confirms actual enrollment for Jan–Apr for all 6 family members listed.
- **Retain this document** with your 2025 tax records. The IRS may use it to verify health coverage compliance under the ACA.
- Employer EIN **94-3320693** (Salesforce Inc) should match any W-2 received from Salesforce for 2025.
