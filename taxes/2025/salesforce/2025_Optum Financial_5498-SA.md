## Key Notes for Tax Filing

- **Form 8889 required:** Use this 5498-SA data to complete IRS Form 8889 (Health Savings Accounts), which must be filed with your federal tax return.
- **Box 2 is total 2025 contributions:** $8,550.00 includes all contributions made in 2025 for the 2025 tax year.
- **Box 3 is zero:** No contributions were made in 2026 for the 2025 tax year as of this statement. If any are made before the 2025 tax filing deadline, an updated 5498-SA will be issued in May 2026.
- **Total 2025 contributions = Box 2 + Box 3** = $8,550.00 + $0.00 = **$8,550.00**
- **2025 HSA contribution limits (IRS):** $4,150 (self-only) / $8,300 (family) / +$1,000 catch-up if age 55+. The $8,550.00 figure aligns with the family limit plus catch-up ($8,300 + $1,000 = $8,300... verify eligibility and employer contributions on Form 8889 Part I).
- **Fair Market Value (Box 5):** $1,199.44 reflects the HSA cash balance plus any investment balance as of 12/31/2025.
- **Account type:** HSA (not Archer MSA or Medicare Advantage MSA).
- **1099-SA:** If distributions were taken from the HSA in 2025, a separate Form 1099-SA would also be issued. Review alongside this form.
- **No corrections:** The "CORRECTED" checkbox is not checked on this form.
