## Tax Filing Notes

- **Form to use:** Report HSA distributions on **IRS Form 8889** (Health Savings Accounts). Attach to Form 1040.
- **Qualified distributions:** If Box 1 distributions were used for qualified medical expenses, they are tax-free and not included in gross income.
- **Non-qualified distributions:** Any portion of Box 1 not used for qualified medical expenses is includible in gross income AND subject to a 20% additional tax (exceptions apply for disability, death, or reaching age 65).
- **Box 3 code 1** means all reportable 2025 distributions are summarized here; fees and investment losses are excluded as they are not reportable.
- **Box 2 = $0** means no earnings on excess contributions — no excess contribution removal issues to report.
- **Corrections:** Any corrections processed before 1/1/2026 are reflected on this form. Corrections made in 2026 will generate a corrected tax document.
- If contributions were also made for 2025, a separate **Form 5498-SA** was issued.
