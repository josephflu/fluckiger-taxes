## Key Concept: Why This Matters

For **covered ESPP shares**, E*TRADE reports a lower cost basis to the IRS (what you paid) but does NOT include the ordinary income component. You must add the **Adjustment Amount** to the 1099-B basis when entering on Form 8949, or you'll be double-taxed.

For **noncovered RSU shares**, ordinary income is already in the 1099-B basis — no adjustment needed.
